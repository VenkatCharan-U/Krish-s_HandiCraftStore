About Us

Krish's Handicraft Store

Krish is a passinate young entrepreneur from Jaipur, Rajastan, who is taking his family's rich tradition of handicrafts into the digital world.

As the first in his family to bring their craft online, Krish combines a love for traditional Rajastani artistry with a modern approch to business.

His store offers an exquisite range of handmade products, each reflecting the culture and craftsmanship of Rajastan, curated for those who appreciate authenticity and heritage.

Krish is dedicated to sharing his family's story and skills with the world, ensuring that the beauty of Rajastani Handicraft reaches evry corner of the globe.
